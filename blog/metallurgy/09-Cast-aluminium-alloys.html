The designation of cast aluminium alloys can be done in two forms.


The numerical designation according to NF EN 1706; that of cast alloys is identical to that of wrought alloys.
Five digits instead of four specify the chemical composition.

The conventional designation according to NF A 57 702 for casting alloys; this is of the alphanumeric type according to NF A 02-004.
The method of production and the metallurgical state are designated by the letter Y followed by two digits in accordance with standard NF A 02-002.
This type of designation is still partially used.

Manifesto: A Journey Through Art and Precision


Welcome to my world. Here begins a journey—not one mapped by the straight lines of logic,
but one that winds through the gears of imagination and the mechanics of ideas.
I am Wallid-Guergour, a craftsman of thoughts and forms, born at the crossroads of France and endless passions.

This site is more than a portfolio; it is an open door to my universe:
drawing, watchmaking, programming, writing,
and the industrial trades.
My world is a tapestry of pixels, sketches, and tiny gears, where every cog tells a story.

From the stroke of a pencil emerges a sketch;
from a line of code, a solution. Between the glow of a
weld and the rhythm of a watch,
I create a space where art meets precision,
where poetry speaks to technique.

This is my laboratory, my workshop, and my library.
Here, you’ll find Python and Haskell tools
designed to automate and innovate,
tailored industrial solutions in welding and metallurgy,
and a unique portfolio that blends creativity with technical mastery.

Behind every image, every line of code,
and every welded structure lies a story.
This manifesto is not an end, but a beginning.
Enter, explore, and be inspired by a vision where art and industry converge in perfect harmony.

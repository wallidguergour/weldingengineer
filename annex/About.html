About Wallid-Guergour | Boilermaker, welder and programmer

About

I’m Wallid Guergour, and I grew up in a world where craftsmanship and creativity merge with industrial rigor. My path is driven by a passion for precision—whether it’s watchmaking, drawing, welding, or coding. I’ve always been fascinated by gears, from those that measure time to those that power the machinery of our ideas.

My work strikes a balance between poetry and precision:

  • Drawing, where each line sketches a dream and captures a fleeting emotion.
  • Programming, where code becomes both language and a source of innovation.
  • Welding and metallurgy, where steel meets flame to shape industrial solutions.
  • Horology, where every tooth matters, every gear is essential, and where even a thousandth of a millimeter can make all the difference.

This diversity of disciplines is the guiding thread of my universe. On this site, you’ll find:

  • Python and Haskell projects to automate, explore, and create.
  • Tailor-made solutions in welding, metallurgy, and industrial design.
  • Artistic and technical creations reflecting my pursuit of both innovation and aesthetics.

I invite you to explore this laboratory of ideas, at the intersection of words, tools, and technologies. I hope you’ll find that spark of inspiration here and feel compelled to bring your own projects to life.

Welcome to my world, where art and industry converge in perfect harmony.
